Design and implementation of a system for T-shirt folding

The inputs to the system are the button that initiates the system sequence and the limit sensors to know that the 2-bar mechanisms have completed their trajectory. The 4 mechanisms are actuated by DC motors with gearbox and are controlled through a driver designed in Proteus Design Suite. The PIC16F877a microcontroller was used as the processing center.
